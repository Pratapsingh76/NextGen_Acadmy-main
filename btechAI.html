Program Curriculum

Comprehensive Introduction to AI and ML:

Students begin with foundational knowledge of data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning, providing a solid base for understanding more advanced concepts.

Programming and Data Science Proficiency:

The curriculum emphasizes proficiency in key programming languages such as Python, crucial for AI and ML applications, along with practical skills in data science including statistical analysis and data visualization.

Advanced Data Management Techniques:

Students learn strategies for effective data acquisition, preparation, and quality enhancement, alongside managing big data with frameworks like Hadoop, Spark, and NoSQL databases.

Statistical and Predictive Analytics:

The program includes training in inferential statistics, predictive modeling, and regression analysis, essential for data-driven decision-making.

Deep Dive into Machine Learning and Neural Networks:

The curriculum covers a range of machine learning techniques such as clustering, classification, and association rule mining, as well as detailed studies of neural networks, deep learning, CNNs, RNNs, and autoencoders.

Specialization in Cutting-Edge AI Fields:

Students have the option to specialize in transformative areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, and advanced topics in generative AI, including prompt engineering and applications of ChatGPT.

Capstone Projects and Live Projects:

Students undertake capstone projects that challenge them to solve complex problems using AI and ML technologies. Live projects provide real-time experience with current technology applications in industry settings.

Internships:

The program integrates internships with leading companies and research institutions, offering students firsthand exposure to professional practice and industry-specific challenges, thereby enhancing their employment prospects post-graduation.
